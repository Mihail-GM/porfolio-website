Bachi:

Develop a system for monitoring and controlling the work process

Creating value in every company is based on the business processes taking place in it. Their rational organization is a crucial factor in making them a competitive advantage for the company. To achieve this, however, business processes need to be thoroughly analyzed and constantly optimized.

The purpose of the project is to give the business a clear idea of ​​what is happening in the company, what tasks should be achieved. The goal is to perform these tasks with exceptional ease. In this way, the manager always has a clear idea of ​​what is going on in the company. With a single click of a button, the manager can receive different information about different projects or different employees. Retain data related to previous projects to analyze an enterprise's business and increase efficiency.

  • Web-based application   
  • Responsive design
  • Flexible and easy to work with
  • 2 type of accounts ( Manager, Employee)
  • Tasks, Calendar, Support system, Reports
Technologies involved:
  • Java EE   
  • Spring Framework
  • Spring Boot
  • Hibernate ORM
  • Thymeleaf
  • MySQL
  • Apache Maven
